### printing temperatures

- Sain Smart TPU 95A: 195°C
- Overtue PLA (matte white): 200°C
- colorFabb nGen (gold metallic): 225°C
- Prusament PETG: No changes to default PrusaSlicer profile

  - Spring Steel Sheet
    - Prusament PETG: -0.380
    - 3DJake PLA: -0.360
